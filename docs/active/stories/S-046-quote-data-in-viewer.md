## Goal

The viewer page shows quote totals alongside the 3D scene. Tier switching updates both the 3D materials and the displayed dollar amounts. The viewer becomes the proposal presentation tool, not just a 3D preview.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Viewer page fetches quote data for all 3 tiers on load
- Side panel or overlay shows: per-zone line items, subtotals, grand total
- Tier toggle updates quote display and 3D scene simultaneously
- Responsive: panel collapses on narrow viewports
